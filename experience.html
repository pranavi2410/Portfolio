Research Internship - IIT Hyderabad

Department: Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering

Domain: Bubble Dynamics and Cavitation

As a student research intern at IIT Hyderabad, I specialized in the study of bubble dynamics and cavitation. My research focused on the thin film thickness between cavitation bubbles and solid boundaries. This project involved:

  • Conducting extensive simulations to analyze the behavior and interactions of cavitation bubbles.
  • Studying the impact of thin film thickness on the overall dynamics and stability of the bubbles.
  • Compiling and presenting the findings in a detailed research report.

This experience honed my skills in simulation techniques and deepened my understanding of fluid dynamics and cavitation phenomena.
